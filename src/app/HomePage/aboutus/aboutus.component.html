Fitness

A broad term that refers to your overall health and well-being, including physical, emotional, and mental health Fitness can also refer to your ability to perform daily tasks without undue fatigue Some components of fitness include:

  • Cardiorespiratory fitness
  • Musculoskeletal fitness
  • Flexibility
  • Balance
  • Muscular strength
  • Muscular endurance
  • Cardiovascular endurance
  • Body fat composition
